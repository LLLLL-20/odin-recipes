Brownies

Go back to homepage

Description:

These delicious brownies will no doubt make you want to bash your head against the wall as the taste is so damn good.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter, melted
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup of walnut halves

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C). Grease a 9x13 pan.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients, spread it into the pan, decorate with walnut halves.
  3. Bake in oven for 20 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out with crumbs,not wet. Cool on wire rack.
